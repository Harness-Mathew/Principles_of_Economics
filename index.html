What is Economics?

Defined as the study of scarcity. Some see it as the study which aims to manage resources. While according to Lord Lionel C. Robbins it is a social science which studies human behaviour as a relationship between end and scarce means

Concepts in Economics

Opportunity cost: The expression of cost in terms of forgotten alternative.

Thinking by the margin: Economist think mostly by the margin, the additional value added or cost in incurred
